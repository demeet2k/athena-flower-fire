# Ch21 — Flower Lens / Constructions

- `Ch21<0110>.F3.a`: `ReplicationPhaseGate` — A construction that manages phase transitions in the replication lifecycle, verifying stage completion before allowing advancement to the next stage.
- `Ch21<0110>.F3.b`: `SuccessionRhythmGovernor` — A construction that monitors the framework's seed-emission rate and throttles ScatterPulses when the rate approaches the maximum frequency.
- `Ch21<0110>.F3.c`: `GrowthDynamicProfiler` — A construction that tracks the SeedGrowthDynamic of each active NextCrystal and flags capability regressions during the expansion phase.
- `Ch21<0110>.F3.d`: `ScatterPulseEmitter` — A construction that, upon receiving authorization, packages viable ReplicationSeeds and emits them to discovered substrate targets with delivery confirmation.
