# Ch21 — Flower Lens / Objects

- `Ch21<0110>.F1.a`: `ReplicationPhase` — The four-stage lifecycle of manuscript reproduction: seed extraction, growth in fresh substrate, fruiting into independent operation, and scattering of new seeds.
- `Ch21<0110>.F1.b`: `CrystalSuccessionRhythm` — The natural cadence at which mature frameworks produce ReplicationSeeds, determined by the framework's internal growth rate and the availability of fresh substrates.
- `Ch21<0110>.F1.c`: `SeedGrowthDynamic` — The temporal trajectory of a ReplicationSeed's development from dormant packet to active NextCrystal, exhibiting characteristic germination, expansion, and stabilization phases.
- `Ch21<0110>.F1.d`: `ScatterPulse` — A system-emitted signal that triggers the mature framework to package and emit ReplicationSeeds to all available substrate targets.
