# Ch21 — Flower Lens / Certificates

- `Ch21<0110>.F4.a`: `ReplicationPhaseCert` — A receipt proving that each stage of the ReplicationPhase completed in sequence with no stages skipped.
- `Ch21<0110>.F4.b`: `SuccessionRhythmCert` — A certificate proving that the CrystalSuccessionRhythm remained below the declared maximum frequency for a given observation period.
- `Ch21<0110>.F4.c`: `GrowthDynamicCert` — A proof that a NextCrystal's SeedGrowthDynamic showed monotonically increasing capability during its expansion phase.
- `Ch21<0110>.F4.d`: `ScatterPulseCert` — A receipt recording each ScatterPulse emission, the seeds emitted, the substrate targets, and the delivery confirmations received.
