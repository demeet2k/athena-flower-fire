# InvZ — Flower Lens / Certificates

- `InvZ.F4.a`: `FundamentalCert` — Receipt proving fundamental frequency correctly extracted, all overtone ratios are integer (or certified algebraic), tonal identity preserved in seed.
- `InvZ.F4.b`: `PhaseRatioCert` — Receipt proving phase ratio encoding is lossless, reconstruction from ratios + fundamental recovers all absolute phases within declared tolerance.
- `InvZ.F4.c`: `SpectralSealCert` — Receipt proving Euler product convergent, zeta-value correctly computed, all prime modes implicitly recoverable from the condensed value.
- `InvZ.F4.d`: `LimitSealCert` — Receipt proving all harmonic series converged, limit values correctly sealed, no divergent or incomplete series remains in the seed.
