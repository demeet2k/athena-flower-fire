# AppK — Flower Lens / Certificates

- `AppK.F4.a`: `AmplificationCutoffCert` — Receipt proving a conflict chain was traced and cut off at the legal maximum length, with the full chain recorded, amplification factors measured, and escalation triggered if applicable.
- `AppK.F4.b`: `CancellationCert` — Receipt proving two conflicts mutually cancelled, the combined deviation is within tolerance, both conflicts are retired, and the cancellation was symmetric.
- `AppK.F4.c`: `QuarantineDecayCert` — Receipt proving quarantine severity followed the legal decay function, never dropped below the floor, and the shard was released only upon formal conflict resolution.
- `AppK.F4.d`: `FirebreakCert` — Receipt proving a firebreak was constructed at the correct radius, all perimeter shards were quarantined, and the conflict wave front was contained within the declared boundary.
