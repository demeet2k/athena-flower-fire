# Ch05 — Flower Lens / Laws

- `Ch05<0010>.F2.a` `CanonicalStabilityInvariance` — The canonical bucket's stability mode must be invariant under small perturbations; any perturbation that destabilizes indicates a latent contradiction.
- `Ch05<0010>.F2.b` `FrontierOscillationBound` — Frontier oscillations must be bounded in amplitude; unbounded oscillation indicates that the quarantine boundary is failing.
- `Ch05<0010>.F2.c` `ParadoxPhaseNonConvergence` — A true paradox's phase portrait must never converge to a fixed point; apparent convergence indicates premature resolution requiring re-examination.
- `Ch05<0010>.F2.d` `ContradictionResonanceDamping` — Contradiction resonance must be actively damped by the containment boundary; undamped resonance risks quarantine breach.
