# InvZ — Flower Lens / Laws

- `InvZ.F2.a`: `FundamentalFrequencyLaw` — Crown collapse to a single harmonic is lawful only when all overtones are integer multiples of the fundamental. The seed frequency is the GCD of all organism frequencies. If the GCD is irrational, the seed carries a transcendental signature.
- `InvZ.F2.b`: `PhaseCoherenceUnderCollapse` — Phase collapse preserves relative phase relationships as ratios encoded in the seed's phase vector. No phase information is lost — it is compressed from absolute angles to ratio encoding.
- `InvZ.F2.c`: `SpectralConvergenceLaw` — The spectral product must converge absolutely for the seal to be well-defined. Convergence follows from the organism's conservation laws: kappa_tot bounded implies spectral density integrable.
- `InvZ.F2.d`: `HarmonicRestLaw` — Every harmonic series in the organism must have reached its convergence value (or be certifiably asymptotic) before crown collapse is legal. Divergent series indicate unfinished expansion — the organism is not ready to seed.
