# AppG — Flower Lens / Laws

- `AppG.F2.a`: `TriadicResonanceLaw` — Triadic resonance is the unique stable attractor of the three-pole system. Any perturbation that breaks 120° separation must be damped within one complete three-phase cycle.
- `AppG.F2.b`: `MediationFlowLaw` — Mercury's flow rate between poles must be bounded: too fast dissolves structure (Salt fails), too slow blocks transformation (Sulphur fails).
- `AppG.F2.c`: `TransformationBoundLaw` — Sulphur's transformation rate must not exceed Salt's crystallization capacity. Excess transformation without fixation = chaos.
- `AppG.F2.d`: `CrystallizationBoundLaw` — Salt's crystallization must not block Mercury's flow. Excess crystallization without transport = rigidity death.
