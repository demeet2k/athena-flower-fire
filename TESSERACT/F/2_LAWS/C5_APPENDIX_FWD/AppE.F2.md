# AppE — Flower Lens / Laws

- `AppE.F2.a`: `ContinuousRotationLaw` — `e^{i·2π} = 1`: one full continuous rotation returns to identity. The fundamental period is `2π/ω`.
- `AppE.F2.b`: `ResonanceRatioLaw` — Two circle gears resonate iff their frequency ratio is rational `p/q`. Irrational ratio = quasi-periodic = never phase-locks = φ-type anti-resonance.
- `AppE.F2.c`: `BeatDampingLaw` — Beat amplitude exceeding threshold must trigger re-synchronization within one beat period. Undamped beats indicate structural decoupling.
- `AppE.F2.d`: `CircumferenceLaw` — `C = 2πr`: the circumference of the circle gear scales linearly with radius. The π-seed's closure law in metric form.
