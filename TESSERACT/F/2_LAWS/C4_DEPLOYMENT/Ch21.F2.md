# Ch21 — Flower Lens / Laws

- `Ch21<0110>.F2.a`: `ReplicationPhaseLaw` — Each ReplicationPhase stage must complete before the next begins; seed extraction must finish before growth, growth before fruiting, and fruiting before scattering.
- `Ch21<0110>.F2.b`: `SuccessionRhythmLaw` — The CrystalSuccessionRhythm must not exceed a declared maximum frequency, preventing the parent framework from exhausting its resources through over-replication.
- `Ch21<0110>.F2.c`: `GrowthDynamicLaw` — The SeedGrowthDynamic must show monotonically increasing capability during the expansion phase; capability regression triggers a growth-failure quarantine.
- `Ch21<0110>.F2.d`: `ScatterPulseLaw` — A ScatterPulse may be emitted only after the parent framework has verified that at least one ReplicationSeed has achieved SeedViability certification.
