# Ch03 — Flower Lens / Laws

- `Ch03<0002>.F2.a` `CorridorPhaseCoherence` — The phase spectrum of truth-flow must remain coherent across corridor junctions; decoherence at any junction triggers corridor re-synchronization.
- `Ch03<0002>.F2.b` `WitnessOrbitalPeriodicity` — Witness availability must follow a periodic schedule whose period divides the corridor's total traversal time.
- `Ch03<0002>.F2.c` `CompressionSymmetryPreservation` — The compression bond must preserve all symmetries present in the source claim; asymmetric compression is a fidelity violation.
- `Ch03<0002>.F2.d` `AdmissibilityWaveformContinuity` — The admissibility waveform must be continuous across the envelope boundary; discontinuities indicate a malformed predicate.
