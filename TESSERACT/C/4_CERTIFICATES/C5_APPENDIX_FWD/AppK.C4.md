# AppK — Cloud Lens / Certificates

- `AppK.C4.a`: `RiskCalibrationCert` — Receipt proving the conflict probability field is calibrated within tolerance `ε`, with the calibration curve, observed vs. predicted rates, and the estimation methodology recorded.
- `AppK.C4.b`: `EvidenceWeightCert` — Receipt proving evidence weights are normalized, each weight is justified by source credibility assessment, and the total weight equals 1.
- `AppK.C4.c`: `BayesianResolutionCert` — Receipt proving the Bayesian conflict resolution converged to a posterior above threshold `τ`, with the full evidence chain and each update step recorded.
- `AppK.C4.d`: `QuorumCert` — Receipt proving the voting quorum was properly assembled (all verifiers independent), votes were tallied correctly, and the majority decision exceeds the required agreement threshold.
