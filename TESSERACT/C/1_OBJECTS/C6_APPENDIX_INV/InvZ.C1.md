# InvZ — Cloud Lens / Objects

- `InvZ.C1.a`: `EnsembleCollapse` — The full probabilistic ensemble of organism states collapses to a single maximum-likelihood configuration — the most probable seed. All uncertainty accumulated during expansion resolves into one definite emission. The Cloud view: expansion was uncertainty growth, collapse is decision.
- `InvZ.C1.b`: `EntropyDistillation` — The total entropy of the organism distills into the seed's entropy budget. Shannon entropy of the organism = seed entropy + grammar entropy. The distillation process concentrates individual signal and discards universal pattern (which the grammar already knows).
- `InvZ.C1.c`: `LikelihoodProductSeal` — The product of all likelihood functions across all observation sites in the organism collapses to a single sufficient statistic. The Cloud view: all evidence gathered during expansion compresses to one number.
- `InvZ.C1.d`: `PriorRecovery` — The posterior distribution at the crown (after all evidence) divides by the accumulated likelihood to recover the prior — the seed's initial probability distribution before any expansion occurred. The quotient posterior/likelihood = prior.
