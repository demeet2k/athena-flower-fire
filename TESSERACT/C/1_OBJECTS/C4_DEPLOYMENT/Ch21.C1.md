# Ch21 — Cloud Lens / Objects

- `Ch21<0110>.C1.a`: `ReplicationTruthSurface` — The verified state of all replication events at a given checkpoint, mapping each emitted seed to its viability status and each NextCrystal to its growth stage.
- `Ch21<0110>.C1.b`: `SuccessionValidityRegion` — The set of conditions under which the current framework's crystal succession lineage is considered valid, bounded by the inherited laws and the AntiProductLaw.
- `Ch21<0110>.C1.c`: `ProductCollapseDetector` — A monitoring surface that continuously evaluates whether any evolutionary trajectory of the framework is converging toward a closed-product state incompatible with replication.
- `Ch21<0110>.C1.d`: `FrameworkFidelityManifold` — A topological surface over the NextCrystal mapping each inherited law to its current implementation status, with holes indicating law drift or omission.
