# Ch20 — Cloud Lens / Objects

- `Ch20<0103>.C1.a`: `MergeTruthSurface` — The verified state of all committed merges at a given checkpoint, forming the ground truth for the collective manuscript's content and provenance.
- `Ch20<0103>.C1.b`: `CollectiveCoherenceManifold` — A topological surface over the AgentOrganism mapping each manuscript region to its current coherence status (fully merged, conflict pending, voice-audited).
- `Ch20<0103>.C1.c`: `VoicePreservationField` — A field over all committed manuscript regions assigning each a per-agent voice-contribution vector, enabling system-wide voice balance analysis.
- `Ch20<0103>.C1.d`: `MergeConflictLocus` — A point in the CollectiveCoherenceManifold where two or more agents' contributions are incompatible and awaiting MergeGovernor arbitration.
