# AppG — Cloud Lens / Objects

- `AppG.C1.a`: `TriadicConfidenceField` — The probability distribution over the three poles' balance state. Narrow distribution = high confidence in balance; wide = uncertain governance.
- `AppG.C1.b`: `ConsensusReliability` — The probability that a dual-certification gate will produce a correct decision given noisy pole inputs.
- `AppG.C1.c`: `PoleFailureProbability` — The probability that any single pole will fail, go offline, or produce unreliable output. Risk surface over the three poles.
- `AppG.C1.d`: `TriadicDegenerationRisk` — The probability that the system will degenerate to a two-pole or single-pole regime through pole failure or weight collapse.
