# Ch03 — Cloud Lens / Laws

- `Ch03<0002>.C2.a` `TruthGradientNonInversion` — The truth gradient along a corridor may flatten but never invert; downstream nodes cannot certify higher truth than upstream sources.
- `Ch03<0002>.C2.b` `WitnessReliabilityDecay` — Witness reliability scores decay toward a neutral baseline over time unless refreshed by successful attestation cycles.
- `Ch03<0002>.C2.c` `CompressionUncertaintyBound` — Compression uncertainty must remain below a declared threshold; exceeding it triggers bond re-evaluation or corridor suspension.
- `Ch03<0002>.C2.d` `ConfidenceIntervalNarrowing` — As additional evidence accumulates, the admissibility confidence interval must narrow or hold steady, never widen.
