# Ch05 — Square Lens / Laws

- `Ch05<0010>.S2.a` `CanonicalSeparationLaw` — No claim may reside simultaneously in the canonical bucket and the frontier bucket; bucket membership is exclusive and deterministic.
- `Ch05<0010>.S2.b` `FrontierQuarantineLaw` — Claims entering the frontier bucket must be isolated from canonical mass within bounded time; delayed quarantine is a containment violation.
- `Ch05<0010>.S2.c` `ParadoxContainmentLaw` — A paradox regime must prevent any contradictory claim from propagating its inconsistency to claims outside its quarantine boundary.
- `Ch05<0010>.S2.d` `ContradictionIsolationLaw` — When a contradiction is detected, both conflicting claims must be moved to the frontier bucket before any downstream operations proceed.
