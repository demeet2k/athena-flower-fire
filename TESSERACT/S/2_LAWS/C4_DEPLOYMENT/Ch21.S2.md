# Ch21 — Square Lens / Laws

- `Ch21<0110>.S2.a`: `ReplicationLaw` — A ReplicationSeed must contain every UniversalInvariant (Ch18), every governance law (Ch17), and every verification protocol (Ch16) needed for independent operation; omission of any component makes the seed non-viable.
- `Ch21<0110>.S2.b`: `CrystalSuccessionLaw` — A NextCrystal must honor all laws inherited from its parent seed while being free to add new laws; it may never weaken or delete an inherited law.
- `Ch21<0110>.S2.c`: `BirthObligationLaw` — FrameworkBirth may be declared only after the NextCrystal has independently passed every ConvergenceTest from Ch19 and produced its own CrossScaleTruthSurface from Ch18.
- `Ch21<0110>.S2.d`: `AntiProductLaw` — The ProductGuard must trigger a structural alert if any modification to the NextCrystal would remove its ability to produce ReplicationSeeds; such modifications are blocked.
