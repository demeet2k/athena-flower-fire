# Ch03 — Square Lens / Laws

- `Ch03<0002>.S2.a` `CorridorAdmissibilityBinding` — Every claim entering a truth corridor must carry evidence sufficient to satisfy the corridor's admissibility predicate at every node.
- `Ch03<0002>.S2.b` `WitnessObligationLaw` — No claim may pass a witness gate without a valid witness attestation; absent witnesses trigger corridor suspension, not silent forwarding.
- `Ch03<0002>.S2.c` `ReplayFidelityLaw` — Any corridor traversal must be replayable from its recorded trace such that the same admissibility verdicts are produced at every node.
- `Ch03<0002>.S2.d` `CertificateChainLaw` — Certificates accumulate along the corridor in a tamper-evident chain; breaking the chain at any link invalidates all downstream certificates.
