# AppE — Square Lens / Laws

- `AppE.S2.a`: `QuarterTurnCycleLaw` — `i⁴ = 1`: four quarter-turns return to identity. The fundamental period of the rotation clock. Anti-aligned phase (`Δθ = π`) produces exact subtraction: `|ψ₁+ψ₂|² = (a−b)²`.
- `AppE.S2.b`: `MixedRadixCarryLaw` — When a digit reaches its modulus, it resets to 0 and increments the next digit. Carry propagation is the nervous system's orbit-advance mechanism. No carry is skipped or absorbed.
- `AppE.S2.c`: `PhaseCoherenceLaw` — Two clocks are phase-locked when their phase difference is constant. Phase-lock is broken by any uncertified carry, missed step, or corridor violation. Re-lock requires explicit synchronization receipt.
- `AppE.S2.d`: `PolygonConvergenceLaw` — The polygon sequence `n·sin(π/n)` converges monotonically to π. Each refinement adds one more side. The circle gear is the limit of all polygon gears.
