# Ch21 — Square Lens / Constructions

- `Ch21<0110>.S3.a`: `SeedExtractor` — A construction that distills the current manuscript system into a minimal ReplicationSeed by identifying and packaging all essential laws, structures, and routing tables.
- `Ch21<0110>.S3.b`: `CrystalGrower` — A construction that takes a ReplicationSeed and a fresh substrate, instantiates the seed's embedded laws, and nurtures the resulting NextCrystal through its initial growth phase.
- `Ch21<0110>.S3.c`: `BirthCertifier` — A construction that applies the full Ch19 convergence test suite and Ch18 invariant verification to a NextCrystal candidate and declares FrameworkBirth only if all pass.
- `Ch21<0110>.S3.d`: `ProductGuardEnforcer` — A construction that continuously monitors the NextCrystal for any modification that would compromise replication capability, blocking such changes before they commit.
