# Ch03 — Square Lens / Constructions

- `Ch03<0002>.S3.a` `openCorridor()` — Instantiates a typed truth corridor between two lattice cells, installing the admissibility predicate at each intermediate node and initializing the replay log.
- `Ch03<0002>.S3.b` `summonWitness()` — Invokes the witness protocol at a gate, collecting attestations from the required witness set and packaging them into a gate-pass token.
- `Ch03<0002>.S3.c` `bindCompression()` — Creates a compression bond by computing the compressed representation alongside a decompression proof, linking both to the source claim.
- `Ch03<0002>.S3.d` `sealAdmissibility()` — Evaluates a claim's evidence against the admissibility envelope's boundary conditions and, upon success, seals the verdict into the certificate chain.
