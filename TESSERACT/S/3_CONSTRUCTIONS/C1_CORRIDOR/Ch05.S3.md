# Ch05 — Square Lens / Constructions

- `Ch05<0010>.S3.a` `sortIntoBucket()` — Evaluates a claim against the canonical admissibility criteria and routes it to either the canonical or frontier bucket, recording the sort decision and evidence.
- `Ch05<0010>.S3.b` `quarantineFrontier()` — Isolates the frontier bucket from canonical mass by erecting access barriers, logging all quarantine boundaries and their activation timestamps.
- `Ch05<0010>.S3.c` `containParadox()` — Applies the paradox regime rules to a set of contradictory claims, constructing a containment boundary and verifying no leakage to canonical mass.
- `Ch05<0010>.S3.d` `filterContradiction()` — Runs the contradiction filter over a claim set, identifying all mutually inconsistent pairs and generating a conflict graph with typed edges.
