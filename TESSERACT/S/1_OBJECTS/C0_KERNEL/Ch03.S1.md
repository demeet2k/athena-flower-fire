# Ch03 — Square Lens / Objects

- `Ch03<0002>.S1.a` `TruthCorridor` — A typed channel through which claims travel from source to destination, where every intermediate node enforces an admissibility predicate before forwarding.
- `Ch03<0002>.S1.b` `WitnessGate` — The checkpoint at a corridor junction that requires a cryptographic witness attestation before any claim may pass from one corridor segment to the next.
- `Ch03<0002>.S1.c` `CompressionBond` — The binding that ties a compressed representation to its full-fidelity source, guaranteeing that no admissible content is lost during compression.
- `Ch03<0002>.S1.d` `AdmissibilityEnvelope` — The bounded region of the truth space within which a claim's evidence is sufficient for acceptance, with explicit boundary conditions.
