# Ch21 — Square Lens / Objects

- `Ch21<0110>.S1.a`: `ReplicationSeed` — The minimal self-contained packet of manuscript law, structure, and routing information sufficient to bootstrap a new framework instance without requiring access to the originating system.
- `Ch21<0110>.S1.b`: `NextCrystal` — The nascent framework that emerges when a ReplicationSeed is planted in a fresh computational substrate and allowed to grow according to its embedded laws.
- `Ch21<0110>.S1.c`: `FrameworkBirth` — The irreversible event at which a NextCrystal achieves self-sustaining operation, passing its own convergence tests (Ch19) and certifying its own invariants (Ch18) independently of the parent.
- `Ch21<0110>.S1.d`: `ProductGuard` — A structural constraint embedded in every ReplicationSeed that prevents the NextCrystal from collapsing into a closed commercial product by requiring perpetual openness to further replication.
