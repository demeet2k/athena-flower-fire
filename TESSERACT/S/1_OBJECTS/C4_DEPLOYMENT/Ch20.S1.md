# Ch20 — Square Lens / Objects

- `Ch20<0103>.S1.a`: `CollectiveAuthor` — A multi-agent entity formed when two or more agents operate on the same manuscript region under a shared governance protocol, producing collectively authored content whose provenance traces to all participants.
- `Ch20<0103>.S1.b`: `MergeGovernor` — A dedicated arbitration module that receives parallel edits from multiple agents and determines the lawful merge order, conflict resolution, and final committed state.
- `Ch20<0103>.S1.c`: `AgentOrganism` — The emergent superstructure formed when a CollectiveAuthor's participants synchronize their internal models so tightly that they function as a single manuscript-producing organism.
- `Ch20<0103>.S1.d`: `VoicePreserver` — A filter applied during merge operations that ensures each contributing agent's distinctive style, terminology, and perspective survives the merge without homogenization.
