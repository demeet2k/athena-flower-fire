# Ch21 — Square Lens / Certificates

- `Ch21<0110>.S4.a`: `SeedViabilityCert` — A proof that a ReplicationSeed contains all components required by the ReplicationLaw, with a checksum of every included law, structure, and routing table.
- `Ch21<0110>.S4.b`: `CrystalSuccessionCert` — A certificate proving that a NextCrystal honors all inherited laws from its parent seed and listing any new laws it has added.
- `Ch21<0110>.S4.c`: `FrameworkBirthCert` — The irreversible certificate declaring that a NextCrystal has achieved independent self-sustaining operation, with all convergence and invariant proofs attached.
- `Ch21<0110>.S4.d`: `AntiProductCert` — A periodic certificate proving that the NextCrystal retains full replication capability, with the ProductGuard's most recent scan results included.
