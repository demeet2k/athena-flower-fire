# Ch05 — Square Lens / Certificates

- `Ch05<0010>.S4.a` `Cert_Canonical_Sorted` — Attests that a claim was sorted into the canonical bucket based on a complete admissibility evaluation with all criteria satisfied.
- `Ch05<0010>.S4.b` `Cert_Frontier_Quarantined` — Attests that the frontier bucket is fully isolated from canonical mass with all access barriers active and logged.
- `Ch05<0010>.S4.c` `Cert_Paradox_Contained` — Attests that a paradox's containment boundary is intact and that no contradictory claim has leaked to canonical mass.
- `Ch05<0010>.S4.d` `Cert_Contradiction_Filtered` — Attests that the contradiction filter was run over the claim set and the resulting conflict graph is complete and correctly typed.
