# InvZ — Square Lens / Certificates

- `InvZ.S4.a`: `SeedEmissionCert` — Receipt proving: seed was correctly derived from crown, hash is collision-free, expansion grammar can regenerate full organism from seed alone, no information was silently dropped.
- `InvZ.S4.b`: `DimensionalIntegrityCert` — Receipt proving: each dimensional reduction step preserved the invariant kernel, no dimension was stripped before its dependents, the 0D residue encodes all 12D information via coefficient packing.
- `InvZ.S4.c`: `MerkleRootCert` — Receipt proving: certificate chain correctly folded, Merkle root matches crown product hash, any individual certificate is extractable and verifiable from the folded tree.
- `InvZ.S4.d`: `CompressionBoundCert` — Receipt proving: compression ratio is within 1/8^n bound, seed capacity sufficient for declared organism complexity, no octave boundary violated, seed is plantable.
