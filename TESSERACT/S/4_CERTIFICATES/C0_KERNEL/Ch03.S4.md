# Ch03 — Square Lens / Certificates

- `Ch03<0002>.S4.a` `Cert_Corridor_Open` — Attests that a truth corridor was correctly instantiated with admissibility predicates installed and verified at all intermediate nodes.
- `Ch03<0002>.S4.b` `Cert_Witness_Present` — Attests that the required witnesses were present at a gate and that their attestations are individually verifiable and collectively sufficient.
- `Ch03<0002>.S4.c` `Cert_Compression_Bound` — Attests that a compression bond is intact, verified by decompressing the representation and comparing it against the original source.
- `Ch03<0002>.S4.d` `Cert_Admissible` — Attests that a claim passed the admissibility envelope's full boundary-condition evaluation with the scored verdict and evidence attached.
