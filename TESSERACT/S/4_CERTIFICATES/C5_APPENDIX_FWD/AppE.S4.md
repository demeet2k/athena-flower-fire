# AppE — Square Lens / Certificates

- `AppE.S4.a`: `QuarterTurnCycleCert` — Receipt proving four quarter-turns returned to identity with no drift or missed step.
- `AppE.S4.b`: `MixedRadixCarryCert` — Receipt proving all carries propagated correctly, no digit exceeded modulus, no carry skipped.
- `AppE.S4.c`: `PhaseLockCert` — Receipt proving two clocks maintained phase-lock for declared number of cycles, with per-cycle phase-difference measurements.
- `AppE.S4.d`: `PolygonConvergenceCert` — Receipt proving polygon sequence converged to π within declared tolerance at declared refinement depth.
