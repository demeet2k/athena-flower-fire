# Ch20 — Fractal Lens / Certificates

- `Ch20<0103>.R4.a`: `RecursiveAuthoringCert` — A depth-indexed proof that merged output at every depth is format-compatible with the merge process at the next higher depth.
- `Ch20<0103>.R4.b`: `FractalMergeCert` — A bottom-up composite certificate proving that all inner merges were finalized before participating in outer merge operations.
- `Ch20<0103>.R4.c`: `SelfGovernanceCert` — A certificate proving that a SelfGoverningCollective's governance modifications satisfied the VoiceProtectionLaw for the governance agent.
- `Ch20<0103>.R4.d`: `DepthVoicePreservationCert` — A multi-depth certificate proving that voice contributions from every recursion level propagated upward without falling below per-depth minimums.
