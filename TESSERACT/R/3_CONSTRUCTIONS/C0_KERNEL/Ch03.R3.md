# Ch03 — Fractal Lens / Constructions

- `Ch03<0002>.R3.a` `recurseCorridorTree()` — Recursively decomposes a corridor into sub-corridors and verifies axiom satisfaction at each level, building a verified decomposition tree.
- `Ch03<0002>.R3.b` `resolveWitnessNesting()` — Traverses the witness nesting chain from leaf to root, verifying termination and collecting base-witness attestations.
- `Ch03<0002>.R3.c` `stackCompressionDepth()` — Applies successive compression layers to a claim, maintaining the bond stack and verifying cumulative fidelity at each depth.
- `Ch03<0002>.R3.d` `testAdmissibilitySelfReference()` — Applies the admissibility predicate to its own definition, producing a self-consistency certificate or a flagged inconsistency.
