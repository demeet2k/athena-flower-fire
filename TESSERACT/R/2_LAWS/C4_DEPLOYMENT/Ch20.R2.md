# Ch20 — Fractal Lens / Laws

- `Ch20<0103>.R2.a`: `RecursiveAuthoringLaw` — A RecursiveAuthoring structure's merged output at depth n must be a valid input to the merge process at depth n-1, with no depth-dependent format exceptions.
- `Ch20<0103>.R2.b`: `FractalMergeLaw` — A FractalMerge must resolve conflicts bottom-up, with inner merge results finalized before they participate as inputs to outer merge operations.
- `Ch20<0103>.R2.c`: `SelfGovernanceLaw` — A SelfGoverningCollective's MergeGovernor must satisfy the same VoiceProtectionLaw that applies to all other agents; governance voice is not exempt from voice thresholds.
- `Ch20<0103>.R2.d`: `DepthVoiceLaw` — Voice contributions from deeper recursion levels must propagate upward through merges without attenuation below the declared per-depth minimum.
