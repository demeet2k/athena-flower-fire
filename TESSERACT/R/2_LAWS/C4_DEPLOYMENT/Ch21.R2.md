# Ch21 — Fractal Lens / Laws

- `Ch21<0110>.R2.a`: `RecursiveReplicationLaw` — Each generation must produce ReplicationSeeds that are at least as complete as those of the parent generation; generational degradation of seed quality is prohibited.
- `Ch21<0110>.R2.b`: `FractalSuccessionLaw` — The lifecycle pattern of each generation must be isomorphic to the parent generation's pattern, with only the scale and substrate differing.
- `Ch21<0110>.R2.c`: `SelfSeedingLaw` — A SelfSeedingCrystal must pass the same BirthCertifier tests that its parent passed before being authorized to emit seeds; no generation may lower the birth threshold.
- `Ch21<0110>.R2.d`: `GenerationalIntegrityLaw` — The GenerationalDepthIndex must be monotonically increasing through the succession chain; no framework may claim a generational depth less than or equal to its parent's.
