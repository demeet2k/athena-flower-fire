# AppE — Fractal Lens / Laws

- `AppE.R2.a`: `NestedClockConsistencyLaw` — The phase of a higher-level clock must be derivable from the phases of its nested sub-clocks. No level may run independently of its children.
- `AppE.R2.b`: `LogPeriodicPhaseLaw` — The fractal phase signature has period `2π/log(φ)`. Departure from this period indicates a different scaling constant governs the recursion.
- `AppE.R2.c`: `CarryCascadeTerminationLaw` — Recursive carry cascades must terminate in bounded steps. Infinite carry = clock overflow = requires modulus extension or orbit restart.
- `AppE.R2.d`: `CircleGearIsomorphismLaw` — Circle gears at different scales are isomorphic up to frequency rescaling. The isomorphism is the φ-lens ScaleBridge.
