# AppG — Fractal Lens / Laws

- `AppG.R2.a`: `RecursiveTriadicConsistencyLaw` — Governance at depth n must be consistent with governance at depth n−1. Internal tria prima must not contradict their parent pole's direction.
- `AppG.R2.b`: `SierpinskiPreservationLaw` — The fractal governance structure must preserve the same balance law at every recursion depth. Depth-specific laws indicate structural defect.
- `AppG.R2.c`: `RecursiveConsensusLaw` — Consensus at any level requires upward propagation of sub-level consensus certificates. No level may certify without sub-level evidence.
- `AppG.R2.d`: `TriadicScaleBridgeLaw` — The triadic ScaleBridge must preserve pole identity across scales. A pole at one scale must map to the same pole type at the next scale.
