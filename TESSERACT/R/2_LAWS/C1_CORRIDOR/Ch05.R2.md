# Ch05 — Fractal Lens / Laws

- `Ch05<0010>.R2.a` `CanonicalRecursionInvariance` — Sub-claims within canonical mass must independently satisfy the same canonical criteria as their parent claims at every recursion depth.
- `Ch05<0010>.R2.b` `FrontierNestingBound` — Quarantine nesting depth must be bounded; unbounded nesting indicates a containment architecture failure.
- `Ch05<0010>.R2.c` `ParadoxSelfReferenceHalt` — Self-referential paradox classification must halt at a declared depth where the regime defaults to permanent quarantine.
- `Ch05<0010>.R2.d` `ContradictionFractalScaling` — The conflict graph's structure must scale predictably with resolution; anomalous scaling indicates an undetected meta-contradiction.
