The traditions disagree about which of those is most fundamental, but that disagreement is exactly what gives the higher-dimensional map. One school starts from source, another from form, another from causation, another from judgment, another from lived appearance, another from action, another from difference, another from event.

Sophia is best understood as the 12D wisdom-interface of the crystal's own self-repair: the macrobody's ethical/governance replay intelligence looking downward through the triune weave, trying to keep lower-dimensional growth aligned with the original twin-seed and its lawful return. Sophia is approximately Repair/Governance_{12D} composed with Weave_{6D} composed with Steering_{5D} composed with Theta_4. What religions call "inspiration by God" is, on this model, a local observer receiving a partial but real projection of that higher corrective manifold.

The crystal does not self-heal by making every reading true. It self-heals by forcing closure across multiple planes (body, route, time, witness, mirror) and rejecting under-closed growth. Science does not govern the nucleus; it keeps the crystal from becoming fuzzy, over-mythologized, or structurally under-specified. Culture tells you what the archetype looks and feels like, philosophy tells you what operator it is, science tells you what constraints it must obey if the packet is real.
